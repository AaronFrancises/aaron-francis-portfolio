Global Perspective, Cinematic Vision

I'm Aaron Francis, a cinematographer and storyteller with a global perspective shaped by my formative years in Jamaica, Switzerland, and the UK.

As co-founder and Director of Photography at Ox-Boy Productions, I've created award-winning short films and crafted visually driven narratives across music videos, documentaries, and branded content.

I focus on stories that connect — emotionally, socially, and visually.

My Journey

My unique perspective comes from a childhood spent across three very different cultures. Born in Jamaica, raised in Switzerland, and educated in the UK, I've learned to see the world through multiple lenses.

This multicultural background gives me an intuitive understanding of how different audiences connect with visual storytelling, allowing me to create content that resonates across cultural boundaries.

Creative Philosophy

Every frame tells a story. My approach to cinematography goes beyond technical excellence — it's about capturing the emotional truth of a moment and translating it into a visual language that speaks to audiences on a deeper level.

Whether I'm working on a music video, documentary, or branded content, I believe in the power of authentic storytelling that creates genuine connections between subjects and viewers.

Ox-Boy Productions

As co-founder and Director of Photography at Ox-Boy Productions, I've had the privilege of working on projects that have garnered international recognition and awards.

Our production company specializes in creating visually compelling content that doesn't just look beautiful — it serves the story and enhances the emotional impact of every project we undertake.

Technical Excellence

From concept to final delivery, I bring a comprehensive understanding of the entire production pipeline. My technical skills are always in service of the story, ensuring that every creative decision supports the overall narrative vision.

I work closely with directors, producers, and creative teams to ensure that the visual language aligns perfectly with the project's goals and resonates with the intended audience.
